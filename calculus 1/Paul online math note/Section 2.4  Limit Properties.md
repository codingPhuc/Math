 Compute the value of the following limit.   
$$
\begin{array}{l} 
\lim_{  x \to  2 }   (3x^2  +  5x -  9)     \\
\lim_{  x \to  2 }3    \lim_{  x \to  2 } x^2 +  \lim_{  x \to  2 } 5 \lim_{  x \to  2 }  x     - \lim_{  x \to  2 }9  \\
3  \cdot  2^2  +   \cdot  5 \cdot 2 -    9   \\
  =  -7
\end{array}
$$
