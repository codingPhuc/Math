$\cos( \circ  + 2\pi) =\cos \circ$ 
$$
\begin{array}{l} \\
\cos  (\circ + 360^\circ )  = \cos \circ  \\
\cos ( \circ -2\pi) =  \cos \circ   \\
\sin (\circ  + 2 \pi)  = \sin \circ  \\
\sin  ( \circ  +  360^\circ ) =  \sin \circ   \\
\sin ( \circ -2\pi) =  \sin  \circ   \\
\end{array}
$$
