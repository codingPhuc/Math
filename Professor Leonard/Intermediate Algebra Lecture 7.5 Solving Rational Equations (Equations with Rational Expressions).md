do this the normal way  
$$
\begin{array} {l}
 \frac{x}{4}+\frac{4}{5}   = \frac{1}{20} \\
 \frac{x\cdot 5}{20}+\frac{4\cdot4}{20}   = \frac{1}{20}
\end{array} 
$$


LCD  = 20 
multiply every terms by the LCD 

$$
\begin{array} {l}
 \frac{x}{4}+\frac{4}{5}   = \frac{1}{20} \\
20\cdot \frac{x}{4}+ 20\cdot\frac{4}{5}   =  20\cdot\frac{1}{20} \\ 
5x+ 16  =  1  \\
5x =  1 - 16 \\
x  =  -3  
\end{array} 
$$

$$
\begin{array} {l}

5x  + 10   + 3x  - 3      =225  \\
8x   =  232 \\

$$


$$
\begin{array} {l}

3( x + 4)  -  4(x   - 3)    = 1   \\
3x  +  12  - 4x   + 12  = 1   \\
-x  = -  23 \\
x = 23
\end{array} 
$$
